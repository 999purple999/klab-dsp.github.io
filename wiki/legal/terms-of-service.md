# Terms of Service — Summary

This article is a plain-language summary of the key points in K-Perception's Terms of Service. It is provided for convenience and does not replace the binding legal document. Always refer to the official Terms of Service published at kperception.com/legal/terms for the authoritative text.

## What the Terms of Service govern

The Terms of Service is a legal agreement between you (or your organisation) and K-Perception. It governs:

- Your right to access and use K-Perception applications and services.
- Your obligations as a user (acceptable use, account security, accurate information).
- K-Perception's obligations to provide the service.
- Subscription terms, billing, and plan changes.
- Intellectual property rights.
- Limitations of liability and disclaimers.
- Dispute resolution and governing law.
- Account termination conditions.

## Who must accept the Terms

All users must accept the Terms of Service when creating an account or (for the Local plan) when first launching the application. By using K-Perception, you agree to be bound by the Terms.

For Team and Enterprise plans, the organisation's administrator accepts the Terms on behalf of the organisation. Individual members are also bound by the Terms through their use of the service.

## Key obligations for users

The following reflects the expected obligations for a product of this type; verify specific clauses against the current Terms at kperception.com/legal/terms.

- **Accurate information:** Provide accurate account information when signing up.
- **Account security:** Keep your password confidential. You are responsible for all activity under your account. Because K-Perception is zero-knowledge, K-Perception cannot reset your vault if you lose your password (only a recovery code can be used).
- **Acceptable use:** Use K-Perception only for lawful purposes. See [Acceptable Use Policy](acceptable-use-policy.md).
- **Age requirements:** You must meet the minimum age requirement in your jurisdiction. Contact support@kperception.com for billing specifics.
- **No circumvention:** Do not attempt to reverse-engineer, circumvent access controls, or abuse the API.

## K-Perception's obligations

The following is derived from the product's technical design; verify specific obligations against the current Terms at kperception.com/legal/terms.

- **Service availability:** K-Perception will use commercially reasonable efforts to keep the service available. Enterprise customers are entitled to the uptime SLA (see [SLA](sla.md)).
- **Zero-knowledge commitment:** K-Perception's architecture ensures that server-side data is ciphertext only. K-Perception will not attempt to decrypt user content.
- **Data handling:** K-Perception will handle personal data in accordance with the Privacy Policy.
- **Security measures:** K-Perception will maintain industry-standard security controls.

## Subscription and billing

- Plans are billed as described on the pricing page. Stripe is the payment processor. Contact support@kperception.com for billing specifics.
- Subscription upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing period.
- Lifetime plans are a one-time purchase granting perpetual access to the features included at the time of purchase. Contact your K-Perception account manager or visit kperception.com for current details on Lifetime plan terms.

## Intellectual property

- K-Perception retains all intellectual property rights in the application and service.
- You retain all rights in your content. K-Perception cannot access your content (it is ciphertext), so no licence to your content is required for K-Perception to provide the service.
- Verify the exact IP clause against the current Terms at kperception.com/legal/terms.

## Account termination

Termination conditions are specified in the Terms at kperception.com/legal/terms.

- **Voluntary termination:** You may close your account at any time from account settings.
- **Termination for cause:** K-Perception may terminate accounts that violate the Terms, engage in illegal activity, or abuse the service.
- **Effect of termination:** Upon termination, your encrypted data may be deleted from K-Perception servers. Export your data before closing your account.

## Limitations of liability

Specific liability caps and carve-outs are specified in the Terms at kperception.com/legal/terms.

K-Perception provides the service "as is" and limits its liability to the extent permitted by applicable law. Given the zero-knowledge architecture, K-Perception has no visibility into content and therefore cannot be liable for content-related claims in the same way a platform that processes content plaintext might be.

## Governing law

Governing law and jurisdiction are specified in the Terms at kperception.com/legal/terms. Contact legal@kperception.com for further detail.

## Changes to the Terms

K-Perception may update the Terms from time to time. Material changes will be communicated in advance. Continued use of the service after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the updated Terms. Contact your K-Perception account manager or visit kperception.com for current details on notification mechanisms and notice periods.
