# Legal Overview

This article provides a guide to the legal documents that govern use of K-Perception. It does not replace those documents — always refer to the official versions published at kperception.com/legal for binding terms.

## Legal documents at a glance

| Document | Who it applies to | Where to find it |
|----------|-------------------|-----------------|
| Terms of Service | All users | kperception.com/legal/terms — Not confirmed in current source; contact support for confirmation. |
| Privacy Policy | All users | kperception.com/legal/privacy — Not confirmed in current source; contact support for confirmation. |
| Acceptable Use Policy | All users | kperception.com/legal/aup — Not confirmed in current source; contact support for confirmation. |
| Cookie Policy | Web and app users | kperception.com/legal/cookies — Not confirmed in current source; contact support for confirmation. |
| Data Processing Agreement (DPA) | Enterprise customers processing EU personal data | On request — see [Data Processing Agreement](data-processing-agreement.md) |
| Sub-processors list | All customers (transparency) | kperception.com/legal/sub-processors — Not confirmed in current source; contact support for confirmation. |
| HIPAA Business Associate Agreement (BAA) | Enterprise covered entities | On request — see [HIPAA BAA](hipaa-baa.md) |
| Service Level Agreement (SLA) | Enterprise customers | See [SLA](sla.md) |
| Security Disclosure Policy | Security researchers | See [Security Disclosure](security-disclosure.md) |

## Terms of Service

The Terms of Service governs your right to use K-Perception, your obligations as a user, K-Perception's limitations of liability, and account termination conditions. All users must accept the Terms of Service when creating an account or installing the application.

See [Terms of Service summary](terms-of-service.md).

## Privacy Policy

The Privacy Policy describes what data K-Perception collects, why it is collected, how it is used, and how long it is retained. K-Perception's zero-knowledge architecture means that note and file content is never collected — the server holds only ciphertext that K-Perception cannot read.

See [Privacy Policy summary](privacy-policy.md).

## Acceptable Use Policy

The Acceptable Use Policy sets out what users are and are not permitted to do with K-Perception. It applies to all plans.

See [Acceptable Use Policy summary](acceptable-use-policy.md).

## Cookie Policy

K-Perception's web application and desktop client use sessionStorage and IndexedDB for vault storage rather than traditional tracking cookies. The Cookie Policy explains what storage mechanisms are used and how to manage them.

See [Cookie Policy](cookie-policy.md).

## Data Processing Agreement (DPA)

Organisations that process EU personal data using K-Perception's services need a DPA in place with K-Perception as the data processor. The DPA is available to Enterprise customers on request.

See [Data Processing Agreement](data-processing-agreement.md).

## HIPAA Business Associate Agreement (BAA)

Healthcare covered entities deploying K-Perception for workflows involving Protected Health Information (PHI) may require a BAA. Not confirmed in current source — contact support for confirmation.

See [HIPAA Business Associate Agreement](hipaa-baa.md).

## Sub-processors

K-Perception uses a small number of sub-processors (third-party services). The current list includes Cloudflare (infrastructure) and Stripe (billing). See [Sub-processors](sub-processors.md) for the complete list.

## Service Level Agreement (SLA)

Not confirmed in current source — contact support for confirmation of SLA terms, measurement methodology, and credit process.

See [Service Level Agreement](sla.md).

## Security Disclosure Policy

If you have identified a security vulnerability in K-Perception, please follow the responsible disclosure process before public disclosure.

See [Security Disclosure Policy](security-disclosure.md).

## Changes to legal documents

K-Perception may update legal documents from time to time. Material changes will be communicated to account holders. The notification mechanism (email, in-app notice, or other) is not confirmed in current source — contact support for confirmation. The effective date is shown on each document.

## Contact

For legal and compliance enquiries: Not confirmed in current source — contact support for confirmation of the correct legal contact email and address.
